About this tool

The BBNJ Agreement Navigator is a free, open-access tool for exploring the text of the Agreement on the Conservation and Sustainable Use of Marine Biological Diversity of Areas beyond National Jurisdiction. It contains the treaty text as adopted on 19 June 2023 (A/CONF.232/2023/4).

The thematic tagging is editorial and does not form part of the official text. For the authoritative source, see the UN BBNJ Agreement.

AI & Marine Environmental Policy

This tool is part of the AI & Marine Environmental Policy research project by Dr Henrique Marcos, Maastricht University. The project investigates how international legal rules on marine genetic resources apply when discovery is AI-enabled.
